EARTH FROM ABOVE

Yann Arthus-Bertrand

Photographic Exhibition

Location : Hubei Museum of Art

Schedule : 25th May - 27th July 2018

Yann Arthus-Bertrand

1976, Yann Arthus-Bertrand travelled to Kenya to do research about the Lions with his wife. It was an opportunity that he saw the beauty of plant on a fire ballon and then he became interested in photography.

For these years, Yann Arthus-Bertrand travelled around the world and took more than 300,000 photos in more than 100 countries.

The exhibition will share some selections of them bringing new horizon of our earth to you. For more information about Yann Arthus-Bertrand, please click the button below.
