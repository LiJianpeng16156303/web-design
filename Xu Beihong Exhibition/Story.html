Gambling

When Mr. Xu Beihong just went to France to study, a foreign student began to look down upon China.Mr. Xu Beihong said righteously to the student: "Since you despise my country, so good, from now on, I represent my country, you represent your country, we will wait until we graduate to see.After that, Mr. Xu Beihong became angry and tried hard to practice and study painting.



Changing art world

In 1929, Xu Beihong hired Qi Baishi as a professor. At that time, Qi Baishi was not valued by the art world. When Xu Beihong came to Qi, Qi Baishi was touched by him: "How can I be a professor like this person?" "You are not only a teacher of the students, but also a teacher of mine!" Xu Beihong said, "Mr. Qi, I need you to help me change the art world!"

Gave a horse to the library

In 1937, a bookstore manager saw that students had no books to read, and wanted to buy books to students. However, he did not have any money. Someone told him that Xu Beihong’s painting was very valuable. He could ask Xu Beihong for help. Xu Beihong heard that he had spent 2 horses. The horse gave it to him and let the students read the book.
