Xu Beihong

Xu Beihong (1895-1953), a Han nationality, was originally named Xu Shoukang, a native of Qiting Town, Yixing City, Jiangsu Province.

Modern Chinese painters and art educators. Having studied western painting in France, he has been engaged in art education for a long time since returning home. He has successively taught in the Department of Art, National Central University, School of Art, Peiping University and Peiping Arts College.

He became president of the Central Academy of Fine Arts after 1949. He is good at characters, animals, flowers and birds, and advocates realism. For more information about Xu Beihong, please click the button below.
